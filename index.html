Wings of Hope – Building a Better Future for Children
Wings of Hope strongly believes in the power of community and solidarity. We are dedicated to supporting children through education, counseling, and inclusion, providing them with access to opportunities that can change their lives. Through our projects, we encourage tolerance, acceptance, and community engagement so that every child has an equal chance.

The 220 Form - 3,5%

Redirect 3.5% of your income tax.

Why to redirect the 3.5% tax?

If you are an employee, you have the option to redirect 3.5% of your income tax towards our projects. This does not involve any additional costs for you, just the redirection of part of the state budget to a cause you support.

The redirected tax is not a sponsorship or a donation, but a legal option through which you can support a foundation, an association, or a non-profit entity like ours.

The only action you need to take is to follow 1 single step below to complete the form 230.

How to redirect 3.5% of income tax?

Step 3: Sign and submit the form
  • The completed and signed form must be submitted by May 25, to the Tax Administration office where you are registered with your residence.

How can you submit the form?

It's simple. You chose through any of the following options:

A. In person at the ANAF counter
B. By mail
  • If you don\'t have time to go in person, you can send the form by mail with delivery confirmation.
  • The fee charged by the Romanian Post for this service is approximately 6.30 RON for an envelope weighing up to 20g.
  • Don’t know which ANAF office you belong to? Here’s a link to the list of ANAF addresses.
C. Online
  • If you have a digital signature, you can complete the declaration online on the ANAF website.
  • If you do not have a digital signature, you can create a user account on the ANAF website.
D. Submission by authorization
  • If you live in Bucharest or another county, you can request an authorization form by Email: blue42wings@yahoo.ca, and a representative will submit the form on your behalf.
